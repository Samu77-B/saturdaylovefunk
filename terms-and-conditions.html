Saturday Love returns in 2026, for our second open air All Dayer. Bringing you the best of Jazz Funk, Soul, Funk, Disco, Soulful House and Rare Groove. Going back to those '70s and '80s club days evoking memories of the "Tribes" you used to "hang with".

We have major live acts, and a some of the UK's favourite independent radio and club DJ's, food & drink stalls, plus pick up a classic vinyl at the record stall. Check out our range of merchandise too.

Our wish is that you all come to enjoy and do have a great time at our Saturday Love All Dayer. Below are a few T&Cs to help the day along.

Ticketing

Tickets are sold through The Friends of Broomfield Park and managed by Ticket Source. On the day you will bring along your ticket acknowledgment and will be issued a relevant wristband which will allow you to move around the venue and come in and out if necessary.

Tickets and wristbands are not transferrable so you and the other person could be ejected from the event if caught with a wristband or ticket you haven't purchased or swopped with someone outside.

Advanced bookings only as there are limited spaces unless otherwise informed. This year we will also have early bird tickets available at great prices.

Anyone under the age of 18 will only be admitted to the event if accompanied by a parent(s) or legal guardian(s).

Refund Policy

You will not be entitled to a refund if you can no longer attend the festival for any reason, if the festival is rescheduled for another date or if an understudy is used in place of the festival's headline act or a headlining act changes from the one(s) advertised on the line-up. You may only be entitled to a refund if the festival is cancelled (but not rescheduled) or has changed location. The refund will be at least the face value of the ticket but does not include the booking fees and there will be an additional admin fee charge* of 3% of the ticket order if you cancel.

Food & Drink

No alcohol can be bought into the main area (The Bowl). Alcohol is available to buy at the event at our fully licensed bars.

You will be asked to leave excess quantities of soft drinks outside the event and you will NOT be allowed to re-enter with your own alcohol once you've entered the site via the entrance gates.

Only soft drinks/water in sealed/unopened bottles & tins are allowed into the festival's main arena and you will not be able to bring in glass bottles of any kind. Broken glass is very dangerous, not only for people who are attending, but for those who use the park after the festival. If you have driven to the festival, please leave all glass/bottles in your vehicle. Opened and unsealed soft drinks must be disposed of before entering the event and placed in one of our available litter bins outside the main arena.

No food will be allowed into the venue as we will have food stalls available for you to purchase from unless you have specific dietary requirements. If so, please can you inform us beforehand. Our stall holders will have alternative food choices including vegetarian and vegan options. Please inform food stallholders of any allergens before purchasing food & drink.

Behaviour

The Saturday Love All Dayer is for everyone to enjoy so we ask that all ticket holders please behave themselves, be tolerant of others. Rudeness, drunkenness, abusive and unsociable behaviour will not be tolerated and you may be ejected from the event area without refund.

When leaving the event please be quiet and respect our neighbours by leaving in an orderly manner.

Rubbish

There will be bins in the area but please take any rubbish you may have brought yourselves home with you.

Dogs and Pets

Unless a guide dog none will be allowed into the event site.

Chairs and other Parafernalia

Please feel free to bring along fold up chairs, groundsheets, blankets and perhaps an umbrella depending on the weather. However please be aware of space as there will be a lot of boogieing, if you want to lay down a groundsheet or set up chairs then perhaps come early to get your space. We suggest more around the outside of the main area.

Event Open/Closure time

Street entry from the main gate (Broomfield Bowl) will be from 1.30pm to those who have pre-paid tickets. The event is expected to close at 10pm on Saturday 21st June 2025. First main artists play from 4pm approximately but running order nearer the time of the event.

ROAR/Security & Safety

Saturday Love Funk and security on the day have the Right Of Admission Reserved.

All persons will be subject to search before entering the gates, if any drugs or illegal substances found then we reserve the right to call the police and we reserve the right to refuse entry.

We will be running a challenge 25 policy. We can refuse to sell age restricted products to anyone who appears to be under the age of 25 years old and does not have an acceptable form of photo identification (ID), like a PASS card. We can be fined for selling age-restricted products to people who are under age and want to keep the event legal for all concerned.

Health Issues

Exposure to loud and excessive music may be damaging to your hearing. Strobe lighting and flash photography may also be used at the event.

There may be midges on site as it is in an open space with trees and grass so you may need insect repellent if you get an adverse reaction to bites.

Also please be aware of bright sunlight and put on the relevant sunscreen level for your skin type.
